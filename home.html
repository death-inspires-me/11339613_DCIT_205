ABOUT US

The University of Ghana's Computer Science department plays a crucial role in the Information Technology age. Emphasizing the importance of computer science in leveraging the benefits of IT for economic advancement, the department focuses on the study and development of applications, software systems, and ethical practices in computing. Central to computer science is the skill of computer programming, and the department aims to produce adept programmers equipped with current techniques and tools.


The department offers undergraduate degree programs, including Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (Single-Major and Major-Minor) and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. The courses provide a diverse range of skills, preparing students for employment in various fields and the computing industry. Additionally, the curriculum serves as a platform for further study or research, exposing students to established and emerging technologies in Computer Science.


The University of Ghana, as one of Africa's prominent public universities, boasts a significant impact on individuals, the region, and the world. With over 38,000 students annually, including around 2.64% in computer science, the university emphasizes turning ideas into impact and confronting global challenges through research. The computer science community at the University of Ghana encourages students to be vibrant and brave, pushing boundaries in a supportive learning environment.


The department embraces modern education methods, offering real-time lectures and personal interactions with professors through online delivery options. This flexibility allows students to customize their degrees to fit their schedules while maintaining academic rigor. The University of Ghana community is defined by a belief in infinite possibilities, unshakable optimism, a connection to others, and a commitment to socio-economic transformation for the greater good. Prospective students are invited to join this journey of exploration and progress.
